About

Jason Koi, Full-Stack Developer

For the past 15 years, I have enjoyed designing, developing, and maintaining web solutions for a wide range of clients. This includes corporate sites for high-profile companies, complex ecommerce systems integrating with external marketplaces and product suppliers, and a custom platform for travel insurance quoting, sales, administration, and policy issuance.

Several years ago, underwhelmed by the bloat and performance of most CMS platforms, my partner and I decided to build our own foundational platform, coding with intentionality and focusing on maximizing performance efficiency. On top of being an immensely enjoyable process, this framework, codenamed Cue, allowed us to build complex business solutions for clients at an efficiency that would not have been possible otherwise. It also powers most of the sites you will visit today.

My approach emphasizes maintainable code, responsive design, and performance optimization. I believe that clean, intuitive design goes hand-in-hand with thoughtful development and strive for both of these in my work.

Below you will find a small sampling of projects that I have worked on over the years, from the large and complex to the small passion project. Thank you for looking.

Projects

GoReady Insurance

goreadyinsurance.com

The GoReady Insurance website nicely encapsulates the type of work that I have been doing for the past 10 years. I was responsible for the entire design and development of the public-facing site as well as the development of many of the systems within.

Under the hood, this site runs on our custom PHP framework, Cue, and is packed with functionality for quoting, product managment, purchasing (including third-party purchasing via API), policy issuance, opportunity tracking and managment, claim managment, an internal task managment system, and content managment.

Note: Branding and brand colors were not part of this project.

Madison Social

madisonsocial.com

Madison Social is a bar and restaurant based in Tallahassee, FL and represents one of the earliest design and development projects that I have worked on that is still available online. This site runs on an earlier incarnation of the Cue framework.

Sugar Creek Foods

getsugarcreek.com

Sugar Creek Foods is a nice example of a brochure-style site that I designed and developed for one of our smaller clients. Also built on Cue, it includes content managment and ecommerce functionality.

Mother Maker

mothermaker.co

Mother Maker is a passion project that I helped create for my wife shortly after the birth of our son. Also built on the Cue framework, I include it here just as an example of good, clean blog and article design.
